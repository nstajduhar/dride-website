Drivers of the world, unite.

We’re a bunch of car fanatics who believe that the vehicles we are driving are unnecessarily primitive and downright dangerous! We’ve designed Dride to change that, and with it, the way we drive. Just as the time when no-one used seat belts rapidly faded we believe that the era of unintelligent cars should be a distant memory, and we are here to bring it about sooner than anyone expects.

Dride Cloud

The Dride cloud is built to help our driving community stay safe.

The cloud gives you your own space to save your driving history and upload or report footage of dangerous drivers within minutes.

Safer and more intelligent community on the road.

Back Face

It’s extremely dangerous to interact with screens on the road which is why we created an indication ring which lights up when communicating with Dride drivers. It’s a simple and intuitive way to receive information on the roads while greatly decreasing distractions while driving.

Front Face

The front of the device has a soft light used to interact with the environment.

Apps

Dride seamlessly integrates with third-party apps making your driving experience even better! With new apps built by our driving community, you are exposed to unique and exciting driving experiences. Anyone can build an app and you get to use them! Do more with safety while driving.


Keeping you safe. Always.
